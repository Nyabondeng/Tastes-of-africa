Injera

Injera is a traditional Ethiopian sourdough flatbread made from teff flour. It is spongy and used to scoop up stews and vegetables.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Mix teff flour with water and ferment for 2-3 days.
  2. Stir and pour onto a hot skillet.
  3. Cook without flipping until bubbles form and it's set.

Ingredients

  • Teff flour
  • Water
  • Time (for fermentation)
