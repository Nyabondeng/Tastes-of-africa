Tagine

Tagine is a North African dish named after the earthenware pot in which it is cooked. Itâ€™s rich, spiced, and slow-cooked.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Brown meat with onions and spices.
  2. Add vegetables and broth.
  3. Slow-cook in a tagine or heavy pot.

Ingredients

  • Lamb or chicken
  • Onions
  • Carrots, potatoes, or prunes
  • Cumin, cinnamon, turmeric
