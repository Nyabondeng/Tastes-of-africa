Bunny Chow

Bunny Chow is a South African street food consisting of a hollowed-out loaf of bread filled with spicy curry.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Prepare a meat or vegetable curry.
  2. Hollow out a loaf of bread.
  3. Fill the loaf with curry and serve with the bread center.

Ingredients

  • Loaf of bread
  • Curry (meat or veggie)
  • Spices
  • Onions, tomatoes, garlic
