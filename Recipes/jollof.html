Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a beloved West African dish, especially popular in Nigeria and Ghana. It's a vibrant mix of rice, tomatoes, and spices.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions.
  2. Fry the mixture in oil.
  3. Add seasonings and rice.
  4. Cook until done.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Seasoning
