Isombe

Isombe is a traditional Rwandan and Congolese dish made from cassava leaves cooked with peanuts and palm oil.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Boil cassava leaves until tender.
  2. Fry onions and tomatoes in palm oil.
  3. Add ground peanuts and cooked leaves. Simmer.

Ingredients

  • Cassava leaves
  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Palm oil
  • Ground peanuts
