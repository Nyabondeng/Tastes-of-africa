Bunny Chow

Bunny Chow is a South African fast food dish consisting of a hollowed-out loaf of white bread filled with spicy curry. It originated in the Indian community of Durban and is now loved nationwide.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Prepare a curry with meat, beans, or vegetables.
  2. Cut a loaf of bread in half and hollow out the inside.
  3. Fill the hollowed bread with curry.
  4. Serve hot with the removed bread as a scoop.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf white bread
  • 2 cups chicken or bean curry
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp garlic and ginger paste
