North African Cuisine

North African cuisine is known for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and Mediterranean influences. Countries like Morocco, Tunisia, and Algeria are celebrated for dishes that include couscous, harissa, and preserved lemons.

Signature Dishes

  • Tagine (Morocco)
  • Brik (Tunisia)
  • Couscous (Algeria)

Common Ingredients

North African dishes feature ingredients like chickpeas, olive oil, dried fruits, and spices such as cumin, cinnamon, and paprika. Tagines are often slow-cooked in clay pots.

Cooking Techniques

Traditional methods include slow braising, steaming couscous over broth, and grilling over open flames. The use of spice blends like Ras el Hanout adds deep flavor to many meals.
